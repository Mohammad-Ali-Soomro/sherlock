═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                         TIMELINE OF EVENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

November 10, 2025 - Monday:
Marcus Chen attended a board meeting at TechVault Industries headquarters located at 
500 Innovation Boulevard. During this meeting, he announced plans to restructure the 
company, which would result in the termination of several senior executives. Board 
member Victoria Blackwood openly argued with Chen about the restructuring plan.

November 11, 2025 - Tuesday:
Chen met with his attorney, Robert Fitzgerald, at Fitzgerald & Associates Law Firm 
(200 Legal Center Plaza) to discuss updating his will. Chen expressed concerns about 
his safety and mentioned receiving threatening emails. He changed his primary 
beneficiary from his wife Elena Chen to the Chen Family Foundation.

November 12, 2025 - Wednesday:
Security footage from TechVault Industries shows James Morrison, former VP of 
Operations (terminated 6 months ago), entering the parking garage at 11:32 PM. 
Morrison was seen near Chen's reserved parking spot. Morrison drives a black 
BMW X5 with license plate 7ABC123.

November 13, 2025 - Thursday:
Elena Chen was seen at First National Bank (450 Commerce Street) accessing the 
family safe deposit box. Bank records show she withdrew $50,000 in cash. That 
evening, Chen's personal assistant, Sophie Williams, reported seeing Elena in 
a heated phone conversation at Riverside Country Club. Elena was overheard saying 
"He'll get what he deserves."

November 14, 2025 - Friday:
Chen attended a charity gala at the Grand Riverside Hotel (1000 Riverside Boulevard) 
from 7:00 PM to 11:30 PM. He was accompanied by Sophie Williams. Security footage 
shows Chen leaving the hotel at 11:47 PM in his Tesla Model S (plate TECH001). 
Elena Chen did not attend the gala, claiming illness.

At 10:15 PM, Victoria Blackwood was captured on traffic camera at the intersection 
of Oak Street and Riverside Manor Drive, approximately 0.5 miles from Chen's residence.
She was driving a silver Mercedes E-Class (plate VB2025).

November 15, 2025 - Saturday (Day of Murder):
- 12:15 AM: Chen's Tesla arrives at his residence (gate camera footage)
- 12:18 AM: Chen enters through front door (doorbell camera)
- 12:45 AM: Motion sensor triggered in backyard (no footage available)
- 1:02 AM: Neighbor Dr. Patricia Hartley reports hearing loud argument from Chen residence
- 1:15 AM: Silent alarm triggered at rear kitchen door (alarm company notified homeowner)
- 1:17 AM: Chen's phone sent text to Sophie Williams: "Need help. Come now."
- 1:23 AM: 911 call from Elena Chen reporting intruder and injured husband
- 1:31 AM: First responders arrive, find Marcus Chen deceased in kitchen
- 6:47 AM: Crime scene officially processed, body transported to county morgue
